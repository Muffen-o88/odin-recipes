Getting ready

Duration of this recipe:

40-60 min


Tools

  • A mixer (like a planetary one), or a whisk
  • A bowl
  • molds, or a banking dish
  • baking paper( only if you want to put the cookies on)
  • a tray
  • spatula

Ingredients

  • 4,4 oz of butter
  • 1/2 of a cup of brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup of normal sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 10,5 oz of choco
  • 1/4 cup of choco powder

Make the cookie

How to do

  1. mix the butter, brown sugar, sugar, vanilla essence(And choco powder, if you are making the choco dough)
  2. put mixed eggs little by little, and mix well
  3. add the flour little by little and mix( it can be done with the hands of in a planetary mixer)
  4. mix the baking powder little by little just to create form
  5. Mix very well
  6. And add the chocolate pieces in the recipe
  7. Now, make little balls on the form with the baking paper (opcional) and put in the preheated oven, let cooking fot 15 or 20 min (250 celsius or 482 fahrenheit)
