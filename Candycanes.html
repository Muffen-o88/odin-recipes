Candy Cane

Hello again guys! Today i will teach you how to do a Candy cane!

It's a christmas recipe, a tradicional candy who everyones knows it and if it's tradicional, you can't fail, so it will be great if you use them for decoration, for a dessert, or even you like, giving this like a souvenir they will love it!(i think so)

Ready? Let's go making Theses goodies!

duration of this recipe:

40-60min

Ingredients

  • 250g of wheat
  • 90g of icing sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 5g of baking powder
  • a bit of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • A food coloring, red color
  • half of a teaspoon of peppermint extracts
  • Crushed peppermint candy canes
  • White sugar for deco

Tools

  • A large bowl
  • A mixer
  • 2 spoon
  • a baking form

How to do

  1. First of all, get all the ingredients
  2. In the large bowl, cream togheter the margarine, white sugar and confectioner's sugar. Beat the egg, vanilla with the peppermint extracts
  3. Combine the flour and salt; stir in the creamed mixture until well blended
  4. Cover or wrap the dough and wait 1 hour
  5. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C ( 375 fehenit) and Grease cookie sheets. Divide dough into halves. Color one half red by mixing in the food color.
  6. Roll a small amount of each dough into a 2 inch long worm. Roll them together in a twisted rope and curve the end like a cane.
  7. Place onto prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
  9. In a small bowl, mix together the crushed candy cane and remaining white sugar.
  10. Roll hot cookies in the sugar mixture. Enjoy!
