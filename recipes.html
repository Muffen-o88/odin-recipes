Ingredients/Tools

First of all, we will need:

  • 4,4 oz of butter
  • 1/2 of a cup of brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup of normal sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 10,5 oz of choco
  • 1/4 cup of choco powder


  • A stoven (duh)
  • A mixer (like a planetary one), or a whisk
  • A bowl
  • molds, or a banking dish
  • baking paper( only if you want to put the cookies on)
  • a tray
  • spatula
